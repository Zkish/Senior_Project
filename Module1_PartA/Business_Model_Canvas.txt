Customer Segments:
* homeowners wanting to save money on rennovations and repairs
* DIY enthusiasts looking for planning tools and answers to questions from reputable sources or even contractors themselves.

Value Propositions:
* instant project estimates (cost, time, difficulty, materials list)
* smart calculators powered by average market prices and location based data
* location based local contractor finder for when the DIY gets too hard to do yourself. 

Channels:
* website

Customer Relations:
* self service (calculators and projections of projects)
* community driven (forums, comment section, and Q&A)
* automated chatbot integration for complex questions on specific projects (long term addition)
