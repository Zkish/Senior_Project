Project Idea: DIY Homeowners do it all website

What the site will offer: 

Project Cost Calculator - tool that will estimate time, cost, and materials needed
monitization of the cost and materials through affiliated sites ex: Amazon, Lowes, HomeDepot.

Group Forum - forum that can do Q&A and general discussions about home improvement projects. 

Helper Finder - Location based contractor reccomendations based on specified project for when the DIY gets too hard.

Each customer will also be able to create a login to use for the site and save information such as past calculations and contribute to forum posts and Q&A.
